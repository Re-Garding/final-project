Books

Why does the Stock Market Go Up?

    - All about the stock market. Super easy to digest (definitions on almost every page)

Girls That Invest

    - All about women empowerment in the world of finance. Provides words of encouragement and walks you through the whole investment process from persepective of women

How to Money

    - The title says it all! Financial literacy from A-Z! A great starter book before diving into some of the jargon-heavy investment books
